Our Certificate & Online Programs For 2023

CAPITAL MARKET
This course is offered in collaboration with SMI (Stock Market Institute). This Course is design to impart practical knowledge of capital markets and its instruments. The capital markets avenues were explained in the theoretical manner in the classroom but the actual real transactions could not be included in the classroom teaching due to various limitation. The Stock Market Institute extended its support for the certificate programme in the Capital market which was for 30 hours duration. It was organized for 2 hour per day scheduled from 10:00-12:00 and spread over 5 days in a week.


FUNDAMENTAL & TECHNICAL ANALYSIS
This Certificate Course is offered in collaboration with SMI (Stock Market Institute). It will train the students on how to analyze with the real data of equity share of various companies. The programme ensured that the students comes of the presumption that the equity trading is a gamble. The students were overwhelmed of the fact that the equity share price can be forecast and determine accurately if they know the FA & TA concept.


DIGITAL MARKETING
The dynamic changes in the business environment need to be met by an equally dynamic curriculum.. Marketing never dies. It evolves . The latest form of marketing is exclusively digital. This course is the missing link between you and the world of digital marketing. Getting a certificate in digital marketing will introduce you to the concepts, principles, applications as well as implications of digital marketing. The course is offered in collaboration with ADDZUT a venture of SMI (Stock Market Institute).


ART OF PUBLIC SPEAKING
Public Speaking, as its name implies, is a way of making one’s ideas public, of sharing these ideas with others. Influentia l ideas have spread around the globe through effective public speaking . The likes of Franklin Roosevelt, Billy Graham, Martin Luther King, Barack Obam a, Aung San SuuKyi and Margaret Thatcher; and more recently Anna Hazare, AravindKejriwal are famous examples. This course will enable students to gras p the Art of Public Speaking and the dynamics that will enable them in applying these aspect s in various public speaking domains. The programme is designed for a period of 45 hour s with two credits for students who score a ‘B’ grade and above. In the event of a student not qualifying the course with a ‘B’ grade and above, the student will receive only a certificate stating the successful completion of the course without, however, any credits.
